Timeline

  • Spring 1996 - We move into NEB 311
  • Spring 1996 - Gremlin comes online
  • Spring 1998 - We buy our first legitimate machine, Chimera
  • November 1998 - Galaxy, our cluster, arrives
  • January 1999 - We move into our newly created office, NEB 229
  • May 2001 - We move again, into NEB 319
  • June 2001 - Centaur arrives
  • Nov 2002 - Added card-swipe so non-officers can use the office
  • Spring 2005 - Our 2 new 1.6 GHz Semprons arrive
  • Spring 2006 - Gigabit deployed to all desktops + servers.
  • Fall 2006 - First metaserver deployed.
  • April 2007 - Last machine of the original set, Gryphon, confirmed dead and disposed of.
  • Spring 2009 - Got closet space courtesy of CS, amiable exchange with JHU Recycling arranged.
  • Fall 2011 - We move to NEB B27
  • Sometime in 2012/2013 - The old systems begin to fail. Plans to create a new cluster are drawn up, involving AFS and Openstack.
  • Sometime in 2013 (before the Fall) - We acquire an extraordinary amount of compute hardware.
  • February 2014 - People other than the sysadmins start to use the cluster again.
  • Summer 2014 - We move to Malone! (Room G67) The cluster stays in NEB B27 though.
  • May 2015 - The cluster is moved to Malone.
